agc_type- [1, 3], 1 is for AGC_DIGITAL_FIXED, 2 is for AGC_DIGITAL_ADAPTIVE, and 3 is for AGC_ANALOG_ADAPTIVE.
agc_level- [0, 31], Sets the target peak |level| (or envelope) of the AGC in dBFs (decibels from digital full-scale). The convention is to use positive values. For instance, passing in a value of 10 corresponds to -10 dBFs, or a target level 10 dB below full-scale.
